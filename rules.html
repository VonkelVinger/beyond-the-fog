Home Rules

What this is

Beyond the Fog is a writing-first project built around one aim: making complex questions easier to see clearly. It focuses on calm, structured explanations that reduce noise rather than add to it.

How it works

Every article follows the same structure. This keeps the focus on clarity rather than style.

  • What’s happening — a clear description of the situation
  • Why it matters — why the topic is relevant
  • Where it gets messy — what is often misunderstood
  • What to watch next — what to pay attention to

What to expect

  • Clear explanations without unnecessary complexity
  • A focus on assumptions rather than opinions
  • Consistency across articles
  • No attempt to predict or overstate certainty

Tools

Some ideas are easier to understand when they can be tested. In those cases, simple tools may be included. These are used sparingly and only when they improve understanding. Most articles do not require them.
